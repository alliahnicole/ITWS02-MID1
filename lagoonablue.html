Lagoona Blue

Lagoona Blue is a major character of Monster High and the daughter of the Sea Monster AKA "The Creature From The Blue Lagoon". She is dating Gillington "Gil" Webber despite their family's bloodfeud.

  1. Lagoona has a very friendly and laid-back personality.
  2. She doesn't sugarcoat her message.
  3. She loves to surf— it's pretty obvious with her pale blue scales and blonde-teal streaked hair.
